About this website

Guide to AAPG [America's Army: Proving Grounds] is a web project challenge inspired by a friend. This website contains guides on becoming better at AAPG.
This website contains material that is strictly compromised of my personal experience in AAPG. Statistics, however, maybe taken from a wiki site or the official AAPG website [americasarmy.com].

America's Army: Proving Grounds (abbreviated to AAPG)

"is the official game of the U.S. Army and part of the highly acclaimed America’s Army game series. This free military game focuses on small unit tactical maneuvers and puts you to the test in a wide variety of new America’s Army maps and AA fan favorites."
